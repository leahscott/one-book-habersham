What is One Book Habersham?

Helen O'Brien

All around the country for the last decade people have been coming together to build community through stories. Habersham County is joining this national movement with One Book Habersham.

One Book Habersham is a community partnership between the Habersham County School System, Volunteers for Literacy, local organizations and businesses.

In February and March people throughout Habersham County are invited to join in this inaugural event with our first featured book, Martina the Beautiful Cockroach by Carmen Agra Deedy. Agra Deedy is an award-winning author and storyteller

who came to Georgia as a refugee from Cuba in the early 1960â€™s.

Martina the Beautiful Cockroach is a folktale that highlights the wisdom of our elders and the importance of family and cultural traditions.

Books will be available at all public libraries and schools throughout the community for you to read, enjoy and share with others.

We invite you to join in this fun approach to building community and promoting literacy in Habersham County.

Storyteller Spotlight

Carmen Agra Deedy

We are very fortunate to have the author of the first One Book Habersham book, Carmen Agra Deedy, in our community for three days this March. She will be speaking and sharing her wisdom with students at three local elementary schools on March 25th, 26th and 27th.

Her storytelling will be the highlight of our community event at Habersham Central High School on Friday, March 27th. You don't want to miss this talented storyteller!
